Our mission

Lumia's mission is to empower and enlighten students on their educational and career journey, with a dedicated focus on the unique needs and aspirations of the Asian demographic, particularly international students. Our goal is to bridge cultural and educational gaps, fostering a nurturing environment where students can thrive academically, professionally, and personally.

At Lumia, we understand that the transition to college and beyond, especially in a foreign country, can be daunting. We are committed to demystifying this process, offering tailored guidance that resonates with the individual experiences and challenges faced by Asian students abroad. Our mission is rooted in the belief that every student deserves access to quality education and the opportunity to pursue their dreams, regardless of their background or geographic location.

We strive to create a community where students feel understood, supported, and motivated to excel. By providing comprehensive services that cover academic advising, career planning, cultural adaptation, and more, Lumia acts as a beacon, guiding students through the complexities of higher education and into successful futures. Our aim is to not only assist students in achieving their immediate educational and career goals but also to instill in them a lifelong love for learning and a global mindset that transcends boundaries.

Our Services

At Lumia, we offer a suite of comprehensive services tailored to the needs of high school and college students, particularly from the Asian community and international backgrounds. Our expertise and personalized approach ensure that every student's journey is not just a path to academic and professional success but also a transformative experience that prepares them for global challenges.

ALL SERVICES

Career Planning and Internship Placement

We offer personalized career planning services, helping students identify their strengths and interests to align with potential career paths. Additionally, we assist in securing internships that provide real-world experience and enhance their professional development.

University Admissions Consulting

Our team specializes in guiding students through the complex university admissions process, from choosing the right institution to crafting compelling application essays. We focus on highlighting each student's unique strengths to maximize their chances of admission to their desired universities.

Scholarship and Financial Aid Navigation

Understanding the financial aspect of studying abroad is crucial. Lumia assists students in identifying and applying for scholarships and financial aid, ensuring they have the necessary resources to pursue their educational goals without undue financial strain.

Cultural Adaptation and Support

For international students, adjusting to a new cultural environment can be challenging. We provide comprehensive support services, including cultural orientation sessions, language support, and ongoing mentorship, to help students seamlessly integrate into their new academic and social settings.
